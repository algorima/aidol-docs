## Slide 3: Solution

**You are the producer.**

AIdol is a season-based simulation where users:

1. **Draft** - Scout AI trainees from a competitive pool (once picked, gone)
2. **Build** - Set group concept, assign positions, design chemistry
3. **Simulate** - Train members, trigger events, watch relationships evolve
4. **Season Result** - Rankings, fan growth, performance scores
5. **Next Season** - New draft, roster changes, accumulated history

> Not another AI chatbot. A producing simulation with stakes.

Core insight from user testing:

*"I'm Min Hee-jin right now."* - User 3, non-fan, fine artist, age 35
