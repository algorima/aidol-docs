## What are you building?

AIdol is a platform where anyone can create, own, and monetize their own AI K-pop idol.

Your AI idol can:
- **Release music** — AI-generated songs and albums
- **Film challenge videos** — Dance content for TikTok/Reels
- **Chat with fans 24/7** — Text, voice, personality-driven conversations
- **Earn like real influencers** — Brand deals, virtual gifts, sponsorships

Creators keep full IP ownership. We're democratizing the virtual idol economy — what costs $10M+ with Lil Miquela, anyone can launch in minutes.

## Why now?

1. **AI hit the inflection point.** LLMs hold personality-consistent conversations. Image/video generation produces idol-quality content. AI music tools are releasing actual hits. The full stack finally works.

2. **K-pop's parasocial economy is massive.** Fans spend $10B+ annually just to feel closer to their idols. AI removes the bottleneck: idols can now engage 1:1 with every fan, around the clock.

3. **Virtual influencers are proven.** Lil Miquela, Imma, Apoki — brands pay them millions. But creation is gatekept by studios with $10M+ budgets. We're opening that gate.

4. **Creator ownership is the trend.** Creators want IP, not just platform reach. AIdol gives them an asset that generates revenue while they sleep.
