## What are you building?

AIdol is an AI idol simulation platform where users become producers — drafting, building, and growing their own K-pop idol groups through seasons.

The core game loop:
- **Draft** — Cast AI trainees from a competitive pool (first-come-first-served)
- **Build** — Set concept, positions, relationships, personality (MBTI-based)
- **Simulate** — 1:N group AI chat with distinct personas, relationship dynamics
- **Season** — Performance evaluation, rankings, growth history

We validated this with 8 in-depth user tests (14 UX research methodologies, cross-user analysis). Users self-defined the category: "This is like being a producer" — one non-fan tester said "I'm literally the CEO of an agency right now" (casting satisfaction 5/5).

Creators keep full IP ownership. We're democratizing the virtual idol economy — what costs $10M+ with Lil Miquela, anyone can launch in minutes.
