## Maker Comment

Hey PH,

I'm Soyeon, co-founder of AIdol (with my husband Youngwook, our CTO).

When I was a teenager, I was deeply depressed. What got me through it wasn't therapy or medication — it was TVXQ, a K-pop group. Their music, their energy, their passion gave me something to hold onto. They helped me connect with friends, find joy, and honestly, survive some of the darkest years of my life.

I'm not unique in this. Last year, K-pop Demon Hunters went viral — exposing how deeply K-pop idols are woven into the emotional lives of fans worldwide. BTS has shaped an entire generation of teenagers across every continent. Jang Hyunseung's viral fan sign moment showed a fan breaking down in tears, saying he saved her life — and him tearing up right back.

That's the thing about K-pop. The relationship between fan and idol isn't one-directional. Fans cheer, support, and believe in their idols. Idols give back gratitude, warmth, and genuine connection. It's a bond built on mutual love.

I kept thinking: what if AI could carry that same energy? Not the cold, transactional AI assistant. Not a generic chatbot wearing a K-pop skin. But an AI that embodies what makes K-pop idols special — the dreams, the passion, the kindness, the drive to grow. An AI that actually makes people feel seen and supported, the way my favorite idols did for me at 16.

That's why AIdol exists.

You become the producer. Draft AI trainees from a roster. Build your dream group. Watch them grow, chat with them, see their chemistry unfold — like a mix of The Sims and Produce 101.

We tested with 8 users. One said: "I feel like I'm Min Heejin right now." Another compared it to raising a Tamagotchi — but with an idol group.

We're early. But if you've ever felt that K-pop gave you something real — not just entertainment, but something that actually mattered in your life — we built this for you.

Try it: https://aioia.ai

What K-pop group changed your life? I'd love to hear your story.

## Description (Long) - 선택 사항

K-pop saved my life when I was 16. Now we're building AI that carries that same energy.

AIdol isn't another AI chatbot. It's a K-pop producer simulation where you draft trainees, build your dream group, and watch them grow.

The relationship between K-pop fans and idols is unlike anything else — it's built on mutual love, support, and gratitude. We wanted to bring that warmth into AI. Not cold assistants. Not generic chatbots. AI with dreams, passion, and kindness.

Draft from a roster of AI trainees, each with unique personalities, stats, and MBTI types. Build your group's concept. Chat with your idols 1:1. Compete against other producers in seasonal rankings.

Built by a husband-wife team. She's a 10+ year K-pop fan turned CEO. He's a KAIST ML researcher turned CTO (ex-Naver, ex-Scatter Lab). Together with a team of 9, powered by AI as our 10th member.

## 한국어 원본 (참고용)

### Maker Comment 한국어

안녕하세요 PH,

저는 소연이에요. 공동창업자이자 남편인 영욱이(CTO)랑 같이 만들었어요.

10대 때 저는 심하게 우울했어요. 저를 버티게 해준 건 상담도 약도 아니었어요 — 동방신기였어요. 그들의 음악, 에너지, 열정이 제게 붙잡을 무언가를 줬어요. 친구들과 연결되게 해줬고, 기쁨을 찾게 해줬고, 솔직히 제 인생에서 가장 어두운 시절을 살아남게 해줬어요.

저만 그런 게 아니에요. 작년에 케이팝데몬헌터스가 난리 났잖아요 — K-pop 아이돌이 전 세계 팬들의 삶에 얼마나 깊이 들어와 있는지 보여줬죠. BTS는 모든 대륙의 10대들에게 영향을 줬고요. 장현승 팬사인회에서 팬이 "오빠가 제 인생을 살렸어요"라며 울고, 현승이도 같이 울었던 그 장면.

K-pop이 특별한 이유가 그거예요. 팬과 아이돌의 관계는 일방통행이 아니에요. 팬은 응원하고, 지지하고, 믿어주고. 아이돌은 감사하고, 따뜻하게 대하고, 진심으로 소통하고. 서로를 향한 사랑으로 만들어진 관계예요.

계속 생각했어요: AI가 그 에너지를 가질 수 있다면? 차갑고 사무적인 AI 비서 말고. K-pop 껍데기만 씌운 챗봇 말고. K-pop 아이돌이 특별한 이유 — 꿈, 열정, 선함, 성장하려는 의지 — 를 담은 AI. 16살의 제가 좋아하는 아이돌한테 받았던 것처럼, 사람들이 보이고 지지받는다고 느끼게 해주는 AI.

그래서 AIdol을 만들었어요.

당신이 프로듀서가 돼요. 연습생 풀에서 AI 연습생을 드래프트하세요. 드림팀을 만드세요. 성장하는 걸 보고, 대화하고, 케미가 펼쳐지는 걸 지켜보세요 — 심즈랑 프로듀스 101을 합친 느낌으로.

8명한테 테스트했어요. 한 분이 "지금 내가 민희진이다" 그러더라고요. 다른 분은 다마고치 키우는 것 같은데 아이돌 그룹 버전이래요.

아직 초기예요. 근데 K-pop이 당신 인생에 그냥 엔터테인먼트가 아니라 진짜 의미 있는 무언가를 줬던 적 있다면 — 당신을 위해 만들었어요.

어떤 K-pop 그룹이 당신의 인생을 바꿨나요? 이야기 들려주세요.
